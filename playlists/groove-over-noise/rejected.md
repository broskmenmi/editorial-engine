# GROOVE OVER NOISE — Rejected Tracks

This file prevents repeated reconsideration of tracks that do not strengthen the playlist identity.

A rejected track may be reconsidered only when:

- a different version or mix materially changes its behaviour;
- the playlist structure has changed enough to create a valid role for it; or
- the original rejection was based on incomplete evidence.
