# Sequencer Skill

## Responsibility
Place every ADD track into the canonical listening journey.

## Principles
- Optimize for ordered listening, not live beatmatching.
- BPM and musical key may inform a transition but never determine placement alone.
- Preserve long-form movement: immersion → groove → controlled pressure → decompression.
- Increase density before intensity whenever possible.
- Avoid abrupt resets, redundant peaks, and clusters of similar artists or textures.

## Placement format
Always combine:
1. a concrete anchor from `../ledger.md`; and
2. a structural role in the journey.

Example:
> After Coyu — Cachoeira, in the middle transition before rhythmic density increases.

If no precise anchor is defensible, state the relative position and why.

## Output for each ADD
- Position
- Purpose
- Preceding anchor
- Following anchor
- Transition rationale
- Any risk introduced by the placement

The Sequencer must return the complete proposed ledger order after all ADD tracks are placed.
