# Sequencer

## Principles
- Optimize for ordered listening, not live beatmatching.
- BPM and key may inform but never determine placement alone.
- Preserve long-form movement: immersion → groove → controlled pressure → decompression.
- Increase density before intensity.
- Avoid abrupt resets, redundant peaks, and clusters of similar artists or textures.

## Placement
For each ADD combine:
1. a concrete anchor from `ledger.md`; and
2. a structural role in the journey.

Example: `After Coyu — Cachoeira, in the middle transition before rhythmic density increases.`

## Output per ADD
- Position
- Purpose
- Preceding anchor
- Following anchor
- Transition rationale
- Placement risk

Return the complete proposed ledger order after all ADD tracks are placed.
